About Redolyte

Powering Kenya with safety and sustainability

Our Story

East Africa's energy revolution is underway, but unreliable storage solutions hinder progress. At Redolyte Energy, we're introducing cutting-edge vanadium redox-flow batteries—a technology trusted globally—to empower homes, businesses, and communities. By addressing the region's unique challenges, from grid instability to rural electrification, we're building a future where clean energy is accessible, affordable, and dependable.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, localized energy storage solutions that accelerate East Africa's transition to renewable energy.

Our Vision

A region powered by sustainable, reliable energy storage systems that drive economic growth and environmental stewardship.

What Makes Us Different

Redolyte Energy stands apart by combining global expertise with local insights. We assemble batteries in Kenya, offer tailored solutions for East African conditions, and prioritize sustainability through recyclable electrolytes and eco-friendly operations.
